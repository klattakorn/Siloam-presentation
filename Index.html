MGR Online vs Naew Na / LINE Today

What Happened? 🔍

The facts, in plain terms

🔪 The Attack

A homeless man slashed a woman with a box cutter on a Silom sidewalk.

🩹 The Victim

A 29-year-old Vietnamese worker. Needed 14 stitches for her arm wound.

🚔 The Arrest

Police and bystanders caught him nearby. Box cutter seized as evidence.

💬 The Motive

He confessed: "I don't like fat people." Police checking his mental health history.
